## 2025-05-15 - [Accessibility Polish]
**Learning:** In static HTML projects without a build system, micro-UX improvements can be grouped by a common theme (like Accessibility) to provide a high-impact "one improvement" that covers multiple gaps (skip links, ARIA labels, aria-live).
**Action:** Always check for icon-only buttons using symbolic characters (‹, ›) and ensure they have descriptive ARIA labels, as screen readers often fail to announce them intuitively.
