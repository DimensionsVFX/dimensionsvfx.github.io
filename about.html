About Dimensions VFX

We are a visual studio crafting cinematic 3D music videos, CGI-driven promos and striking cover art for artists and labels worldwide.

Our Story

Founded by a collective of directors, 3D artists and VFX specialists, Dimensions VFX blends a streetwise sensibility with luxury production values. We focus on visual storytelling that elevates music and artist identity.

Our Approach

We combine concept development with technical craft — procedural 3D, photoreal compositing and cinematic lighting — to deliver visuals that feel both bold and timeless.

Collaborations

We work directly with artists, labels and creative agencies. Our remote-first workflow lets us coordinate across time zones while keeping quality and deadlines tight.

Meet The Team

Our team includes Directors, 3D Generalists, Compositors and Motion Designers. We believe in clear communication, iterative feedback, and delivering work that artists are proud to release.
