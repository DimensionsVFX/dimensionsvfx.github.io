About Dimensions VFX

Dimensions VFX is a visuals studio focused on music-first design. Our work sits between street energy and cinematic polish – tailored for artists who care about how their sound looks.

Based in India and working worldwide, we collaborate remotely with artists across the USA, UK, Australia, Nigeria and beyond. From early concepts to final grade, we keep everything tight and intentional.

Focused on artists

  • Remote-friendly workflow with clear feedback rounds
  • Delivery formats matched to your platform – YouTube, IG, Spotify, etc.
  • Visual direction that respects your sound and brand
