Frequently Asked Questions

What information should I include in my inquiry?

Share your track (or brief description), desired timeline, budget range (if applicable), and any visual references or style preferences. The more details, the better we can assist.

Do you work with artists internationally?

Yes! We work with artists worldwide. Our team is remote-first and experienced in coordinating across time zones. Most of our communication happens via email and calls.

What are your typical turnaround times?

Turnaround depends on project scope and complexity. We'll provide a timeline estimate once we understand your brief. Most projects take 2–6 weeks.

Do you offer revisions?

Yes, we include revision rounds in our standard process. The exact number depends on your project agreement.
